About the project

Brooklyn, disco era. Felice "Felix" Bolsoniro has just been made Capo of the Galliano crime family. In his world, reputation is everything, and mistakes can be deadly. But Felix is harboring a secret that could destroy him: he's in love with Johnny, a bartender at the city’s hottest discotheque. Over the course of one night, Felix dodges the demands of his volatile mob boss uncle, Carmello Galliano, while Johnny pushes for a peaceful life together, out of harm’s way. When their worlds violently collide in a moment of reckless abandon, Felix is forced to make a terrible choice. One that may haunt him forever. Starring Fabrizio Brienza and Mike Starr. Based on a true story. Sex, drugs, and disco dancing. With a body in the trunk.
