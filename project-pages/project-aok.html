About the project

Partnered with The Grimes Brothers and The Jackal Group to produce 6 live-action short films for AOK YouTube channel. Plugged production and budget management capacity into Grimes Brothers' creative engine. Delivered one short film per month under strict $5,000 budgets. Primitive Technology (Parody) became one of the most-viewed live-action videos on the channel.
