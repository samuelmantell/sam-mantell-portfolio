About the project

A cinematic portrait of people experiencing homelessness in Los Angeles. Disco'd is a documentary that captures the moment-to-moment uncertainty and endless instability of life on the streets. Immersive and intimate, highlighting issues of displacement, affordable housing, addiction, consumerism, and sanitation, Disco’d examines the nature and complexities of homelessness.
